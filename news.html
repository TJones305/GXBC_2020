Competiton News

County Competition Deadline now passed

The deadline for county competitions for the upcoming 2021 season has now closed. If you are usure if you have entered please speak to Giles Brisbourne (Men's) or Julie Skelly (Ladies). For all that have entered we wish you the very best of luck for a successful year

National Competitions Deadline 1st March 2020

Entries for the National Competitions ran by Bowls England are still open until Monday 1st March 2021. Should these not go ahead due to COVID-19 a full refund will be offered. If you entered competitions last year that didn't take place, check the entry lists in the competitions portal on Bowls England's website (link at bottom of page) in case you entry has been carried forward.

Social News

At present due to the impact of COVID-19 and the current Government guidance the clubhouse remains closed. As soon as the restrictions are eased the club will review what social events are possible in the future. In mean time remember: "Stay at Home, Protect the NHS, Save Lives"

Member News

The Green Team

Even through these difficult and heavily resticted times our green maintence team have soldiered on and have been carefully tendering their loving care to our bowls green. Once we are back out playing in the coming months please show the team your thanks and appreciation
